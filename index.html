Tom Brady

The Greatest NFL Player Ever

Thomas Edward Patrick Brady Jr. (born August 3, 1977), also known as Tom Brady, is an American football quarterback for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League (NFL). He spent the first 20 seasons of his career with the New England Patriots, where he was a central contributor to the franchise's dynasty from 2001 to 2019. Brady is widely considered to be the greatest quarterback of all time. Brady was largley succesful with the New England Patriots, where he bagged 6 rings.
